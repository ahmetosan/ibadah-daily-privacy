Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Ibadah Daily ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your email address and display name (if you choose to share them).
  • Location data: We use your device's location to provide accurate prayer times and Qibla direction. Location is used only when you use these features and is not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Usage data: Information you create in the app, such as prayer tracking, reading group memberships, commitments, and bookmarks.
  • Group data: Group names, descriptions, and membership information you create or join.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide prayer times based on your location
  • Enable Qibla direction and masjid finder features
  • Sync your data across devices (groups, commitments, prayer tracking)
  • Display your profile and group contributions to other group members
  • Show relevant advertisements to support the app (see Section 5)

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud). We use industry-standard measures to protect your information. Data is transmitted over HTTPS.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and cloud storage
  • Sign in with Apple / Google Sign-In: Account authentication
  • Google AdMob: To display ads. AdMob may collect data for ad personalization. We use non-personalized ads where possible for privacy compliance.

Each service has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing Google's and Apple's privacy policies.

5. Advertising

Our app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can control ad personalization in your device settings. We use content filtering to ensure ads are appropriate for our app.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access and update your profile information within the app
  • Delete your account and data by contacting us
  • Disable location access in your device settings
  • Opt out of personalized ads in your device settings

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates when changes were last made. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
