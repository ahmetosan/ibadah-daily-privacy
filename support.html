Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get accurate prayer times?

Ibadah Daily uses your device location to calculate prayer times. Make sure location access is enabled in Settings → Ibadah Daily → Location. You can also adjust the calculation method in the app under Prayer Settings.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → scroll to the bottom → tap "Delete Account." This will permanently delete your account and all associated data including prayer tracking, group memberships, and bookmarks.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Profile → Support the App → tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom of the page.

How do I join a reading group?

You can join a group by tapping an invite link shared by the group creator, or by entering the invite code in the Groups tab.

Is my data private?

Yes. We do not track you or sell your data. We use non-personalized ads only. Your prayer data and group information are stored securely with Firebase. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

How do I remove ads?

You can support the app with a one-time tip through Profile → Support the App. As a thank you, ads will be removed.
